About Our Gates

Thank you for your interest in Windows Guards INC, your trusted provider of advanced security solutions for homes and businesses in New Jersey. Whether you're looking for window guards, doors, gates, or other physical security products, we're here to help.

Site assessment and consultation:

  • Before installing a gate, it's essential to assess the site where the gate will be installed. This involves examining factors such as the terrain, existing structures, utilities, and access points.
  • During the consultation phase, the customer's requirements and preferences are discussed. This includes determining the type of gate needed, any specific features or functionalities required, and any aesthetic preferences.
  • The site assessment and consultation help ensure that the gate installation meets the customer's needs and is compatible with the site conditions.

Custom design and planning:

  • Based on the site assessment and consultation, a custom design is created for the gate.
  • The design takes into account factors such as the size and shape of the gate, the materials to be used, and any additional features such as automation or security systems.
  • Planning involves determining the logistics of the installation, including scheduling, obtaining any necessary permits or approvals, and coordinating with other contractors if needed.

Professional installation by trained technicians:

  • The installation of the gate is carried out by experienced and trained technicians.
  • The technicians have the knowledge and expertise to install the gate correctly and safely, following industry best practices and safety standards.
  • They ensure that the gate is securely mounted, properly aligned, and operates smoothly.

Testing and quality assurance:

  • After the gate is installed, thorough testing is conducted to ensure that it functions as intended.
  • This includes testing the opening and closing mechanisms, checking for any issues or malfunctions, and ensuring that all safety features are operational.
  • Quality assurance measures are taken to verify that the gate meets the company's standards of quality and craftsmanship.
