Legal

Legal Rights and Responsibilities

At Windows Guards, we are committed to providing high-quality gates and exceptional service to our customers in Jersey. As part of our dedication to transparency and professionalism, we want to ensure that our customers are aware of their legal rights and responsibilities when purchasing and installing gates from us.

Customer Rights:

  • Quality Assurance: We guarantee the quality and durability of our gates. If you encounter any defects or issues with your gate due to manufacturing or material faults, we will work diligently to resolve the problem promptly.

  • Warranty Coverage: All our gates come with a warranty to protect against manufacturing defects. The specific terms and duration of the warranty may vary depending on the type of gate and components involved. Please refer to your warranty documentation for detailed information.

  • Professional Installation: Our team of experienced professionals will ensure that your gate is installed correctly and safely. We adhere to industry best practices and safety standards to minimize the risk of accidents or damage during installation.

  • Customer Support: We are committed to providing excellent customer support throughout your experience with us. If you have any questions, concerns, or issues regarding your gate or installation, our friendly and knowledgeable staff are here to assist you.

Customer Responsibilities:

  • Compliance with Regulations: As a customer, it is your responsibility to ensure that your gate installation complies with all relevant local regulations, codes, and ordinances in Jersey. This may include obtaining any necessary permits or approvals before installation.

  • Maintenance and Care: To ensure the longevity and optimal performance of your gate, regular maintenance and care are essential. This includes lubricating moving parts, inspecting for wear or damage, and cleaning the gate and surrounding areas as needed.

  • Safe Usage: It is important to use your gate safely and responsibly. Follow all manufacturer instructions and warnings, and exercise caution when operating the gate, especially if it is automated or motorized.

  • Notification of Issues: If you encounter any issues or concerns with your gate, please notify us promptly. Timely communication allows us to address any problems and ensure your satisfaction with our products and services.
